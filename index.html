The Orbis School

About

Top schools in Pune have always attracted students from all over the country and abroad, and in such an environment The Orbis Schools are the preferred choice of parents seeking admission to good schools in Pune. The school's name, Orbis, which comes from orb, is a metaphor for the world. And our aim as educators is to groom students in harmony with global progress. Today, The Orbis Schools are recognized as one of the best schools in Pune.
